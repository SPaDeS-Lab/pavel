Security and Privacy in Distributed Systems Lab

SPADES Lab, short for Security and Privacy in Distributed Systems Lab, is a research facility focused on exploring the intricacies of digital security, privacy protection, and distributed systems. Our team of researchers works to develop practical solutions to real-world challenges in these areas, aiming to enhance the resilience of digital infrastructures and safeguard sensitive information. SPADES Lab seeks to advance the understanding and capabilities of security, privacy, and distributed systems, contributing to a safer and more reliable digital environment.
